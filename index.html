Apps

Carrot Core Creations exists to make software with a point of view. Not everything needs to be massive, noisy, subscription-shaped, or trying to become a platform.

Sometimes a good app should just do its thing well. Sometimes a good game should just feel nice to touch. That is the lane.

What this is

Carrot Core Creations is an independent software label focused on making apps and games that feel personal, direct, and well finished. The aim is not to ship everything. The aim is to ship the right things with care.

  • Apple-native by default Products are designed to feel at home on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and the rest of the Apple ecosystem.
  • Focused scope Each project starts with a clear core idea and avoids unnecessary clutter around it.
  • Polish matters Good motion, clean interactions, readable interfaces, and satisfying details are part of the product.
  • More on the way The library starts with Twenty48 and Knowitpad and grows over time with future apps and games listed here.

What to expect

  • Original apps and games built with a native feel in mind
  • Clean product pages for each release and future launch
  • A growing list of projects instead of a crowded front page
  • Designs that lean warm, readable, and intentionally simple
  • Software that tries to be charming without being gimmicky
  • Direct access to each project from this landing page
  • More carrots than corporate jargon
