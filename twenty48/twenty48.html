Twenty48

Calm, tactile, dangerously replayable

The world's most innocent-looking time sink, Twenty48 is a polished swipe-and-merge puzzle game built for quick sessions that somehow turn into much longer ones. Slide the board, combine matching tiles, chase higher numbers, and try not to say "just one more round" too many times.

What it is for

Twenty48 is for the kind of puzzle session that feels simple in the first move and strangely serious a few minutes later. It works well as a quick mental reset, a score chase, or a calm little ritual you keep coming back to.

  • Classic merge puzzle play Swipe the board, combine matching tiles, manage the grid carefully, and push toward that next big merge.
  • Readable, tactile game feel Smooth controls, crisp board feedback, optional haptics, and a layout designed to feel clear at a glance.
  • Stats worth checking Track best score, moves, highest merge, games played, and your current streak while you keep improving.
  • Visual styles you can switch up Change the background and tile look to keep the board feeling fresh without overcomplicating the game.

Capabilities

  • Built around the familiar 2048-style tile merging loop
  • Supports multiple backgrounds and tile themes
  • Includes a stats sheet for score and streak tracking
  • Offers optional haptics for a more tactile feel
  • Designed to stay clean, readable, and responsive
  • Local-first play with no account required
  • Made to feel at home on Apple platforms

Why it exists

A lot of mobile games are noisy, overloaded, or designed to pull attention in every direction at once. Twenty48 goes the other way. It keeps the core puzzle clean and lets the satisfaction come from the board itself.

The goal is simple: make a version of this number puzzle that feels polished, calm, and genuinely nice to play, whether you have thirty seconds or thirty minutes.
