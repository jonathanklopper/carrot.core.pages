Mergemathica

A clean number-merging puzzle with classic play, math-flavored modes, and quick runs on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Mergemathica is a number puzzle about smart swipes, satisfying merges, and pushing one move farther than last time. Play the classic board, unlock new board sizes and merge modes, track your best runs, and keep playing across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Classic merges with a math twist

Mergemathica keeps the satisfying swipe-and-merge loop, then adds math-flavored modes and board sizes for quick runs that still leave room for better decisions.

  • Classic number-merging gameplay Swipe the board, combine matching tiles, and keep pushing one move farther than last time.
  • Extra merge modes Play Tripled+, Squares, Primes, Fibonacci, and Factorials when you want a different kind of run.
  • More board sizes 4x4, 5x5, and 6x6 boards are available in the full game.
  • Not just another 2048 clone Apple didn't want another 2048 game, so this is a similar concept, but not the same.
  • Progress that follows you Undo, saved stats, Game Center achievements, and leaderboard support help every run count.
  • Apple Watch play Play on Apple Watch with synced game state across your devices.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs Built without third-party analytics SDKs.
