Grave Mistake

Hold the line against the horde

Grave Mistake is a tiny Apple Watch zombie survival shooter about one bad bridge, one stubborn Dude, and a horde that keeps coming. Move with the Digital Crown, tap to fire, grab ammo, and survive as long as you can before the undead break through.

What it is for

Grave Mistake is built for quick, tense, readable runs on your wrist. Every second is about holding the line, watching the lane, and deciding when to spend ammo before the horde gets too close.

  • Apple Watch-first survival Spin the Digital Crown to move, tap to fire, and play short arcade runs designed for a small screen.
  • Weapons that change the run The pistol stays reliable, the minigun burns through bullets, the laser sweeps the lane, and rockets clear trouble fast.
  • Scarce ammo, rising pressure Collect ammo before it passes, dodge holes, switch weapons, and make every shot matter.
  • Tactile score chasing Optional haptics, saved progress with Continue, high score tracking, and compact cutscenes keep each run punchy.

Capabilities

  • Apple Watch-first arcade gameplay
  • Digital Crown movement
  • Tap and hold weapon controls
  • Pistol, minigun, laser, and rocket launcher
  • Ammo pickups and weapon switching
  • Haptic feedback with an on/off setting
  • Game state saving with Continue
  • High score tracking
  • Pixel-art zombie chaos
  • Short intro and game-over cutscenes

Why it exists

Grave Mistake turns a tiny survival loop into a quick undead panic machine: the bridge is collapsing, ammo runs dry, zombies slip past, and one grave mistake can end the whole run.

The goal is simple: make a fast Apple Watch arcade game that feels readable, tactile, and worth one more try.
