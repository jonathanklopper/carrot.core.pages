Privacy Policy for Knowitpad

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

Knowitpad is a lightweight macOS application designed for editing plain text files locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how Knowitpad handles data.

1. Data Collection

Knowitpad does not collect any personal data.

  • No account is required
  • No user identifiers are collected
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No data is transmitted to external servers

2. Local File Access

Knowitpad only accesses files that you explicitly choose to open or save.

  • Files remain stored on your device
  • Knowitpad does not upload, share, or transmit file contents
  • All processing occurs locally on your Mac

3. Data Sharing

Knowitpad does not share any data with third parties.

4. Tracking

Knowitpad does not track users.

  • No cookies
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No advertising frameworks

5. Third-Party Services

Knowitpad does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

6. Security

Since Knowitpad does not collect or transmit data, there is no user data stored or processed outside of your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Knowitpad does not collect any personal information from users of any age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date.
